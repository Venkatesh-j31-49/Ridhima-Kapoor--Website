Ridhima Kapoor

Ridhima Kapoor, born on December 10, 2014, in Chennai, is a national champion junior golfer from Bengaluru, India. Currently a Grade III student at DPS Bengaluru North, she has been training at To The Tee Academy since age 7. Ridhima's dedication, talent, and passion for golf have earned her numerous accolades and made her a standout in junior golf. She aspires to win Olympic gold for India and succeed in the LPGA. Known for her remarkable skills, sportsmanship, and positive attitude, Ridhima is an inspiration to her peers and is poised to make a significant impact on the international golf stage.

Beyond the Green

Ridhima Kapoor's dreams are vast and ambitious. With her eyes firmly set on the future, she aims to represent India on the global stage, including the Commonwealth Games and the Olympics. Her vision embodies her belief that relentless hard work, discipline, and an unwavering focus can lead to extraordinary success.

A Shining Talent

Ridhima's journey is marked by her outstanding achievements. As a national champion junior golfer from Bangalore, she exemplifies the power of dedication and perseverance. Her name is celebrated in the golfing community, reflecting her consistent excellence and remarkable skill.

A Beacon of Inspiration

Ridhima Kapoor is more than just a golfer; she is a beacon of inspiration for young athletes and dreamers everywhere. Her story proves that age is no obstacle to greatness and that with dedication and hard work, anyone can achieve incredible heights.
